Why I Travel

Why I Travel

Travel has shaped who I am. Growing up in Guatemala and El Salvador, serving a mission in Brazil and studying in the U.S., I learned early on that the world is big, beautiful, and full of perspectives different from my own. Every trip teaches me something new, about the world and about myself.

My Travel Philosophy

  1. Immerse in the culture
    • Try the local food, always
    • Learn a few words in the local language
    • Visit local markets, not just tourist spots
  2. Connect with people
    • Talk to locals whenever possible
    • Travel with friends or make new ones on the road
  3. Document the journey
    • Take photos that tell a story
    • Keep a travel journal
    • Share experiences with others
  4. Be present
    • Put the phone down sometimes
    • Embrace the unexpected
